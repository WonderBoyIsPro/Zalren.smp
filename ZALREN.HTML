Discord Rules 🔥

1. Be Respectful

No bullying, hate, or rude behaviour. Treat everyone kindly.

2. Show Mutual Respect

Treat all players with basic respect. No racism, discrimination, targeted harassment, or toxic behavior.

3. No Spam or Drama

Don’t flood chats, troll, or start arguments.

4. Keep It Clean

No NSFW content, offensive/racial jokes, or heavy swearing.

5. Use Channels Right

Post things in the right place (e.g., help in help, casual talk in general).

6. No Ads or Self-Promotion

Don’t promote your Discord, YouTube, etc., without media permissions.

7. Respect Staff

If a staff member tells you something, listen. Use tickets for problems.

8. No Cheating or Exploits

Don’t use or share cheats, hacks, or bugs. Play fair.

9. Use Tickets for Support

Use the ticket system for bans, reports, or payments instead of pings.

10. No Impersonating

Don’t pretend to be staff or fake information.

11. Follow Discord ToS

You must follow Discord's Terms of Service and Guidelines.

12. No IRL or Cross-Trading

Trading in-game items for real money or across other games is prohibited.

13. No Doxxing or Threats

Never share private info. Threats lead to a permanent ban.

14. Don’t Ping Staff to Troll

Pinging staff for fake problems or to troll will lead to punishment.

Rule-breaking will lead to mutes, kicks, warnings, or bans.
